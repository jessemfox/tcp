About Terra

Terra Capital Partners is a leading provider of "gap financing"— specifically, mezzanine loans, bridge loans, b-notes and preferred equity — to the U.S. commercial real estate industry. We are owned and operated by a team of seasoned professionals who combine hands-on real estate experience, credit skills and sophisticated capital markets expertise. With strong relationships in the financial and real estate sectors, we are well-positioned to take advantage of opportunities in our niche.

Our income funds are designed to generate a low-volatility income stream of consistent cash distributions to our investors. Every investment is subject to rigorous analysis with an emphasis on property and market fundamentals and durability of cash flow.
